About

Who I Am

I'm Dr. Saad Asif — a registered physiotherapist based in Karachi, Pakistan, specializing in sports injuries and musculoskeletal conditions.

My approach is simple: evidence-based treatment, clear communication, and a genuine focus on getting you back to doing what you love — whether that's sport, work, or daily life without pain.

I also run an educational Instagram page — @drsaadphysio — where I break down physiotherapy in simple, everyday language so everyone has access to quality health information.
